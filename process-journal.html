Week  5     9.6-9.13

Previously on assessment 1, our group was focusing on parcel delivery. Based on the previous research and analysis, a primary problem has been revealed. Loss of parcel is a fairly often happening issue in the delivery business. There are various reasons lead to loss of the parcel. First and the most frequently happening case is sending to a wrong destination. As drop and go is quite common in Australia, if the parcel is dropped at a wrong place, everyone is going to ignore it. As a result, the original recipient can hardly track or even get the parcel. The number of transitions is another reason contributes to the loss of item. Based on an estimation, more than 80% of operation is done manually. Those operations include receiving, put away, storage, picking, and shipping (Dewa, Pujawan & Vanany, 2017). Various human errors happened in warehousing operations give rise to picking the wrong item or losing the item. Precisely, the more station the parcels go through, the higher possibility they get lost, vice versa.

The three of us talked about the feedback we got in the first assignment. I found that our common result for the first assignment is that users are very concerned about whether the package will be lost during the delivery process.So we decided to use parcel lost as our main problem to solve.

One of the team members suggested that the drone can be used to send direct mail between friends and friends, so that you can send the goods directly to the place you want to send without going through the post office.Another team member suggested that the error can be directly detected in the warehouse. Using the drone to transmit in the warehouse can reduce the chance of manual error. The probability of success of the machine is often much greater than the probability of artificial success. If it can be reduced in warehouse The error rate of the package, which can be found from the root of the problem to the solution.My idea is that you can use the drone delivery directly send the parcel instead of manual delivery.Users can book the package to be delivered and the drone to be used on the mobile phone, and can reserve the time to deliver the goods, and the drone delivery has the advantage that the delivery speed is very fast, if there is emergency needed mail and parcels, it can be quickly delivered from the post office to the address you want。







Week  6     9.13-9.20

Currently, most of the express/ extra charged deliveries provide “signing for the parcel” service. This feature can be adapted to my idea as every parcel should be insured during the delivery.

The strength of the drone delivery: Drone delivery instead of manual transport can reduce labor costs； Delivery time can be greatly reduced； Users can reduce the time to pick up the parcel and get the goods directly at home； Viewing the package delivery at any time with mobile phone ease the manual work； Drone delivery of parcels reduces the possibility of parcel loss；

The weakness of the drone delivery: The cost of the drone will be higher than the labor cost; The flight path of the drone needs to be tested repeatedly; The drone may encounter uncontrollable conditions during the flight; The drone may not operate successfully on big rainy days;

When we tested our mockup we used two test methods, one is think aloud another is interview,Think aloud is to collect the real time response from the participants. As they are asked to speak out their thought when they are moving through our user interfaces, we can immediately jot down the parts that make them confused. After recording their feeling and reaction, we are able to do changes based on the collected response.This method can gain a detailed story behind users’ experience through open-ended questions. It is effective to get deeper information.

Week  9      10.4-10.11

In the ideation stage, we came up 3 concepts which address the loss of parcel in different aspects. Now, we are going to select one and further develop it into the next level. Despite two of them are going to be put down here, the functions and features inside will be brought to the chosen one if possible.

After a series of evaluations, we finally choose Secure Delivery as our to-be-developed concept. There are 2 reasons that made it better than the others. First, the key findings from assessment 1 revealed that the majority of losing parcel was because of the Courier. It is true that Parcel Distribution and Direct Delivery reduce the risk of parcel loss during the transition between warehouses, but Secure delivery is a direct solution to the problem. Second, it is probably not realistic to apply Direct Delivery when the parcel is traveling a long way. The battery of drones and weather changes becomes a huge problem yet they are some kind of engineering problem. Precisely we would like to go for the solution which is most likely to achieve in the coming future. As a result, we chose Secure delivery for further development.AutoV is designed to solve the problem that is currently in the delivery industry. Below is our original concept flow chart and design. Based on the user evaluation, key findings include typography, flow, function, layout. First, Attendances mentioned the contrast between fonts and background is not enough. We ,therefore, re-correct the color of textbox in order to emphasize the fonts. Next, we did some changes on layout according to what participants revealed. For example, in the “choose you parcel “ page, we altered the distance between each options which made the layout more understandable. Besides, we united the fonts into Montserrat because it has been being the first place in the ranking of “most frequently used font” in UI design. Extracted from our interview, font size is one of the problems. So we altered the fonts size as well. Second, in our refining, we didn’t altered much on the flow, we just change the title name from “ order a drone” to “start delivery”. As former is confusing that participants thought they could buy a drone through our apps. Last, participants mentioned the bar menu is a repeated function and can be removed. So we abandoned the switching function in bar menu and only kept one home button on it.
