DISCUSSION

In the ideation stage, we came up 3 concepts which address the loss of parcel in different aspects. Now, we are going to select one and further develop it into the next level. Despite two of them are going to be put down here, the functions and features inside will be brought to the chosen one if possible.

After a series of evaluations, we finally choose Secure Delivery as our to-be-developed concept. There are 2 reasons that made it better than the others. First, the key findings from assessment 1 revealed that the majority of losing parcel was because of the Courier. It is true that Parcel Distribution and Direct Delivery reduce the risk of parcel loss during the transition between warehouses, but Secure delivery is a direct solution to the problem. Second, it is probably not realistic to apply Direct Delivery when the parcel is traveling a long way. The battery of drones and weather changes becomes a huge problem yet they are some kind of engineering problem. Precisely we would like to go for the solution which is most likely to achieve in the coming future. As a result, we chose Secure delivery for further development.

After evaluating the screen-based interfaces with users, it shows that the APP meets the needs of receivers meanwhile helps eliminate the lost of parcel and improve the efficiency of the parcel delivery service. The screen-based interfaces can still be improved in terms of the quality of the user interfaces. For the next step, we are going to do more evaluation on our chosen concept. It is need more researches and improvement for a better development. Based on the evaluation of our chosen concept. We will focus on working with interact interface.In the next assignment, we will use the knowledge we have learned in the class, make more changes on the interface, to make it more close to daily life using.
